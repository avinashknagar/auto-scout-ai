## Market Insights

In Delhi-NCR between Rs 2.6–5.0L, dealer-backed platforms (Spinny/Cars24) keep pricing tight around segment averages, so the true bargains are usually (a) cars that are at/near the minimum of their competitive tier-year set, or (b) models with weaker resale (e.g., Kwid) priced aggressively. The best value here is concentrated in compact hatchbacks (Tiago/Grand i10) and base sedans (Amaze/Dzire) where cross-shopping pressure is high and discounts show up clearly versus competitive averages. For buyers, prioritize 1-owner cars with verifiable service history, inspect for repaint/accident and suspension wear (Delhi roads), and treat outside-fitted CNG/brand-exit risks as negotiation points rather than automatic deal-breakers if documentation and condition are strong.
