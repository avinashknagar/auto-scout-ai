## Market Insights

In Delhi-NCR’s Rs 1.3L–5.0L used-car band, Maruti/Hyundai listings typically carry a resale premium, so true bargains are the ones that beat both their own model-year averages and their segment/tier competitive averages (not just one of them). The strongest value pockets in this dataset are (1) Tata Tiago/Tigor where some cars are priced far below the competitive set, and (2) select Maruti tallboys/sedans where you get liquidity + low running costs without paying peak market prices. Be cautious with discontinued/low-support models (Datsun, Hyundai Eon) even if discounts look large—treat them as ‘price-first’ buys and negotiate harder after inspection. For any short-listed car, prioritize accident/paint checks, service history, tyre age, and a test drive focused on clutch/AMT behavior and suspension noise before paying a token.
